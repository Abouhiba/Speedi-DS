Unmistakably Speedi.
Built for Saudi quick-commerce.

One identity for every Speedi surface — the red, the flat honesty, the savings-first attitude — executed for a market defined by Keeta, Ninja and HungerStation.

1 · Brand idea

Delivery is table stakes — everyone brings the order to the door. Speedi's promise is what's left in your pocket afterwards. Every design decision should express: fast without feeling rushed, smart with money, effortlessly local.

You Save. We Deliver. الكل يوصّلك، بس حنا نوفّر عليك

The Arabic slogan leads in Saudi media; the English line mirrors its structure (concession → counterpunch) rather than translating it.

2 · Logo

English wordmark — leads on English-language surfaces
Arabic wordmark — leads on Arabic-language surfaces
  • The red wordmark is unchanged — it is the strongest recognition asset. Use the vector component (SpeediLogo, lang="en"|"ar"); never redraw.
  • The identity is bilingual: the English wordmark leads on English surfaces, the Arabic wordmark (سبيدي) leads on Arabic surfaces. Never mix marks on one surface hierarchy — the secondary language mark may only appear as the paired lockup on collateral.
  • English mark top-left, Arabic mark top-right on any collateral; centered only in constrained spaces.
  • Clear space = the x-height unit on all sides; minimum print width 2.5 cm.
  • Color variants: full color (default), single-color negative on dark, black positive/negative for one-color reproduction.

English: Hanken Grotesk — a premium modern grotesque; sharper and more confident than Poppins while keeping open, friendly forms. Arabic: Alexandria — a geometric contemporary face that pairs with it naturally and reads native to Saudi digital products.

5 · The campaign tag system

Promotions are the centerpiece of the product. One tag primitive covers every campaign family, color-coded so shoppers learn the language:

20% OFF Flash Deal Buy 1 Get 1 Bundle & Save Coupon Cashback Free Sample Spin & Win Free Delivery
  • Tinted style on white surfaces; solid style over imagery.
  • Maximum one tag per product card — the strongest offer wins.
  • Tags never replace the price; the red price remains the hero.

6 · Voice

Do

Short, confident, money-first. "You Save. We Deliver." · "⃁ 25 back on your first three orders." · Title Case for sections, sentence case for body.

Don't

No exclamation-point hype, no emoji in product UI, no "we're excited to announce", no literal translation between languages — localize the idea.

7 · Motion

  • Fast without feeling rushed: 120ms taps · 200ms cards/fades · 320ms pages. Easing cubic-bezier(0.2,0,0,1) — decisive, no bounce.
  • Press feedback: scale 0.97. Entrances: 12px rise + fade.
  • Overshoot/celebration easing is reserved for campaign moments (spin-the-wheel, scratch cards, coupon reveals) — never navigation or checkout.

8 · Surfaces & components

  • Flat system: white cards (12px radius, no shadows) on warm-neutral #F6F6F4; hairline #EBEBE8 dividers.
  • One item card everywhere — the unified ProductCard (grid + row variants) is the only product card, across category, search, recommendations, related items and brand pages.
  • Hero and promo banners remain backend-managed drag-and-drop slots — design the frame, not the artwork.
  • Profile header carries the completion ring with reward-linked task chips.

9 · Iconography & imagery

  • Iconsax-style line icons, linear weight by default; icons inherit text color.
  • Product photography: cutouts on the neutral tile #F4F4F2; campaign artwork warm and saturated; no gradients as UI decoration.
  • No emoji, no hand-drawn glyph substitutes.
